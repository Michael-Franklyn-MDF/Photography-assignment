About This Website

Hello, I am a passionate photographer dedicated to capturing the beauty of the world through my lens. With years of experience in various photography styles, I strive to create stunning images that tell a story and evoke emotions. I go by the name Michael David Franklyn.


Welcome to Captured Moments! This website showcases a collection of stunning photographs that capture the beauty of the world. Each image is carefully selected to evoke emotions and tell a story. Whether you're looking for inspiration or simply want to appreciate the art of photography, we hope you enjoy exploring our gallery.


Photography is a way of feeling, touching, and loving. What you have caught on film is captured forever. It remembers the little things, long after you have forgotten everything.

Welcome to my collection of favorite moments, where every photo tells a story and every story is a memory cherished.
